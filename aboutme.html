JAMANALANG

BIOGRAPHY

Jam Manalang is a passionate and creative 21-year-old currently living in the Philippines. With a keen interest in film-making and video editing, he pursued a multifaceted path as an aspiring director, filmmaker, and all-around visual video provider. Over the years, he has honed his skills in industry-standard tools such as Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, After Effects, Photoshop, Lightroom, and Blender, allowing him to create visually compelling content across various platforms.


Currently pursuing his education as a third-year student in a four-year program, Bachelor of Science in Digital Media and Interactive Arts. Jam balances academic life with creative projects and content creation. His academic interests have included typography, branding, information architecture, and web designing, with practical experience in tools like Figma for building interactive prototypes.


In his personal life, Jam is a Filipino born in Cainta, Rizal. Spending half of his life in San Pedro City, Laguna. A son to a half Chinese-Spanish-Filipino mother and Marine Engineering father. Currently residing and finishing his college in Iloilo City, Iloilo with her mother.
